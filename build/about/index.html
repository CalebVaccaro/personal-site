About Me

Hello! I'm Caleb Vaccaro, a dynamic game developer with a passion for crafting immersive virtual experiences, emphasizing physically engaging interactions.

My Journey

I started my journey in game development 8 years ago, and since then, I have worked on various projects ranging from small indie games to larger collaborative efforts. My primary focus is on physical immersion and mixed reality, but I have experience in various areas of game development, including Game/Player Physics, Networking, and constructing pipelines to easily distribute products.

In my free time, I enjoy growing various plants and dancing to keep me on my toes, which I believe contributes to my out-of-the-box thinking in game development.
